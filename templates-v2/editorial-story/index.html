Editorial Archetype

A Story-Driven Website That Builds Trust Before the Pitch

Long-form narrative sections, journal-like rhythm, and subtle visual pacing.

Chapter 1

The Problem

Use natural language and empathy to frame client pain points, similar to a feature lead in a newspaper front page story. Start broad, then narrow to the local service challenge.

Sidebar

Quick stats, quote callout, or featured testimonial.

Chapter 2

The Method

Walk through the process like a follow-up story with subheads and practical detail.

Chapter 3

The Result

End with evidence, outcomes, and a direct but non-pushy CTA.
