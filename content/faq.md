# FAQ Copy — Missoula Website Co

Use these across Homepage, Pricing, Services, or a dedicated FAQ page.

## FAQ Entries

### 1) How quickly can you launch a website?
Most projects launch in a few weeks, depending on scope, content readiness, and revisions. For simpler builds, we can often move faster. We’ll give you a realistic timeline after intake.

### 2) Do you write the website copy too?
Yes. We can write full page copy, refine your existing content, or collaborate on a hybrid approach. Our copy process focuses on clarity, trust, and conversion.

### 3) Do you work only with Missoula businesses?
We primarily serve Missoula and nearby Western Montana communities, but we can support businesses outside the area as well.

### 4) What happens after the site launches?
We continue with hosting, maintenance, updates, backups, and support based on your plan. You won’t be left to manage technical issues on your own.

### 5) Can you improve my existing site instead of rebuilding?
In many cases, yes. We’ll assess your current setup and recommend whether an optimization path or a rebuild is the better investment.

### 6) Is SEO included?
Every plan includes foundational local SEO setup. Ongoing SEO and content growth support can be included in Growth/Pro plans or add-ons.

### 7) Will my site work well on mobile?
Absolutely. We design mobile-first because most local service searches happen on phones. Clear structure and fast performance are core priorities.

### 8) What industries do you work with?
We work with many local service and professional businesses: contractors, home services, wellness, legal, financial, and more.

### 9) How much do websites cost?
Pricing depends on scope, features, and support level. Our plans are designed to be transparent, and we’ll recommend the best-fit option after reviewing your needs.

### 10) Do you offer ecommerce or booking features?
Yes. Booking, payments, and ecommerce can be included based on your selected plan and scope.

### 11) Can I request edits after launch?
Yes. Ongoing edits are included based on your monthly plan, and additional requests can be scoped as needed.

### 12) What do you need from me to get started?
Usually: basic business details, services list, goals, brand assets (if available), and any existing website/login access. If you don’t have everything ready, we can still get started.
