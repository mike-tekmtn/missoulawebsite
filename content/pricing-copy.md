## Local Positioning Notes (Pricing)
- Emphasize “local partner” value, not just commodity pricing.
- Pair pricing with support reliability and responsiveness — strong differentiator in Missoula market.
- Keep wording honest and specific; avoid overpromising timelines or rankings.
- If pricing changes often, keep page copy flexible and move actual numbers into editable components.
