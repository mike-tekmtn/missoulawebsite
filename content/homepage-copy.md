## Local Positioning Notes (Homepage)
- Use phrases like “Missoula-area,” “Western Montana,” and “local service businesses” naturally throughout.
- Include service-area references in key sections (e.g., Missoula, Lolo, Frenchtown, East Missoula, Bonner-West Riverside).
- Use local proof elements: recognizable landmarks, testimonials from local industries, and region-specific customer intent language.
- Keep tone practical and direct; avoid heavy agency jargon.
- Prioritize trust language: response times, real support, clear scope, transparent pricing.
