# Forge Method Coaching Brand Kit

- **Template:** `fitness-coach`
- **Vibe:** Motivational, high-energy, focused
- **Voice:** Action-oriented, concise, transformational
- **Typography:** Outfit + Inter
- **Imagery Direction:** Training sessions, form coaching, progress snapshots
- **UI Treatment:** Strong funnel CTA, bold typography, metric proof blocks

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#0A0A0A`
- Accent: `#F472B6`
- Secondary Accent: `#F97316`
- Text/Light: `#F5F5F5`

## CTA Copy Style
- Primary CTA should be explicit and task-focused.
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction (learn more, view options, etc.).

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** keep imagery and iconography consistent with profession context.
- **Don't:** reuse generic stock visuals across unrelated professions.
