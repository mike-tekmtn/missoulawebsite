# BrightPath Dental Brand Kit

- **Template:** `dental-clinic`
- **Vibe:** Clean, calm, reassuring
- **Voice:** Warm, professional, family-safe
- **Typography:** DM Sans + Inter
- **Imagery Direction:** Smiles, exam room, care moments, bright whites/blues
- **UI Treatment:** Rounded cards, soft shadows, simplified booking CTA

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#F8FAFC`
- Accent: `#3B82F6`
- Secondary Accent: `#14B8A6`
- Text/Light: `#0F172A`

## CTA Copy Style
- Primary CTA should be explicit and task-focused.
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction (learn more, view options, etc.).

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** keep imagery and iconography consistent with profession context.
- **Don't:** reuse generic stock visuals across unrelated professions.
