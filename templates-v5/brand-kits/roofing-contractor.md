# North Valley Roofing Brand Kit

- **Template:** `roofing-contractor`
- **Vibe:** Rugged, trustworthy, fast-response
- **Voice:** Direct, practical, emergency-ready
- **Typography:** Inter + Manrope
- **Imagery Direction:** Storm damage, crews on rooflines, close-up material textures
- **UI Treatment:** Strong KPI tiles, dispatch tags, high-contrast CTA buttons

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#0B1220`
- Accent: `#22D3EE`
- Secondary Accent: `#F59E0B`
- Text/Light: `#E5EDFF`

## CTA Copy Style
- Primary CTA should be explicit and task-focused.
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction (learn more, view options, etc.).

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** keep imagery and iconography consistent with profession context.
- **Don't:** reuse generic stock visuals across unrelated professions.
