# Granite Legal Group Brand Kit

- **Template:** `law-firm`
- **Vibe:** Credible, composed, premium
- **Voice:** Confident, precise, strategic
- **Typography:** Playfair Display + Source Sans 3
- **Imagery Direction:** Courtroom textures, desk details, serious portraiture
- **UI Treatment:** Editorial spacing, serif headlines, restrained accent usage

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#F5F1EA`
- Accent: `#8B5E34`
- Secondary Accent: `#111827`
- Text/Light: `#6B7280`

## CTA Copy Style
- Primary CTA should be explicit and task-focused.
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction (learn more, view options, etc.).

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** keep imagery and iconography consistent with profession context.
- **Don't:** reuse generic stock visuals across unrelated professions.
