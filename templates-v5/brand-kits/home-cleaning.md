# FreshNest Cleaning Brand Kit

- **Template:** `home-cleaning`
- **Vibe:** Friendly, bright, organized
- **Voice:** Helpful, clear, stress-reducing
- **Typography:** Nunito Sans + Inter
- **Imagery Direction:** Before/after rooms, tidy spaces, team in action
- **UI Treatment:** Bento proof cards, trust badges, quick-quote pathways

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#111827`
- Accent: `#A78BFA`
- Secondary Accent: `#60A5FA`
- Text/Light: `#F8FAFC`

## CTA Copy Style
- Primary CTA should be explicit and task-focused.
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction (learn more, view options, etc.).

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** keep imagery and iconography consistent with profession context.
- **Don't:** reuse generic stock visuals across unrelated professions.
