# Peak Climate Systems Brand Kit

- **Template:** `hvac-service`
- **Vibe:** Technical, dependable, local-coverage
- **Voice:** Clear, service-led, operational
- **Typography:** Inter + IBM Plex Sans
- **Imagery Direction:** Tech diagnostics, vents, crews in service vans
- **UI Treatment:** Map-led sections, zone chips, availability indicators

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#052E2B`
- Accent: `#34D399`
- Secondary Accent: `#A3E635`
- Text/Light: `#E6FFFA`

## CTA Copy Style
- Primary CTA should be explicit and task-focused.
- Secondary CTA should reduce friction (learn more, view options, etc.).

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** keep imagery and iconography consistent with profession context.
- **Don't:** reuse generic stock visuals across unrelated professions.
